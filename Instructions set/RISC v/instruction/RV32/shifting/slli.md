 shift left logical by immediate
 
 `slli rd, rs, imm5` that translates to `shift rs left by imm5 bits for rv32`

 `slli rd, rs, imm6` that translates to `shift rs left by imm6 bits for rv64`

since its a logical left shift the shifted least significant bits are in the filled with zeros
